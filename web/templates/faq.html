Frequently Asked Questions

If I don't have a .csv with the best solutions, what should I do?

Don't worry. It's not a required field. You can submit your problem anyway.

Am I required to fill in the name of the variables and objectives?

No, if you don't want to fill the names, we can take care of it for you.

Why did my problem fail?

Your problem can fail for many reasons. Most likely, the .jar is not suitable.

If your problem persists, please contact us, so we can help you.

Why do I have to insert my email?

You will be notified of the progress of your optimization problem.

How do I build a suitable jar?

You must create a class named "pt.iscte.es2.Plugin" that must implement one of JMetal's Problem interfaces:

  • AbstractIntegerProblem
  • AbstractBinaryProblem
  • AbstractDoubleProblem
  • Problem
